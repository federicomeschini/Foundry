# Target Analysis: META Group (Technical Implementation Manual)

## 1. Corporate Identity and Strategic Units
META Group is an international "Research Accelerator" and boutique investment firm (founded ~30 years ago) that operates as a high-density bridge between scientific research and commercial markets.

### Core Business Units:
*   **META Consulting:** Specializes in "Knowledge Valorisation." They design and manage innovation programs for public authorities. They are the **Lead Contractor** for the European Commission’s **Horizon Results Booster (HRB)**.
*   **META Investment (META Ventures):** The venture capital arm managing ~€100M+ in cumulative assets. They manage public-private co-investment vehicles (e.g., INGENIUM).
*   **META Academy:** The training arm implementing the **Research Angels™** methodology.

META Investment operates as a "Smart Money" investor, often co-investing with business angels and regional development funds.

### Active & Successor Funds:

*   **NanoVelos (LifeSci):** Early-stage drug delivery.

## 3. The "Research Angels™" Methodology: Technical Logic
This proprietary framework is the "Software Requirement" for how META coaches researchers.

### Phase I: The Competence Phase (TRL 1-4)
*   **Goal:** Bridge the "European Paradox" (High research, low market uptake).
*   **Activities:** Problem/Solution framing, UVP building, and **Intellectual Asset Management (IAM)**.
*   **Decision Lens:** "No use, no impact." Validating if a scientific result has a clear societal or market pain point.

### Phase II: The Financial Phase (TRL 5+)
*   **Goal:** Transition from research project to commercial entity.
*   **Activities:** Business modeling (Lean Canvas), investor readiness training, and cap table structuring.
*   **Incentive Model:** META often uses a **"Sweat Equity" model**, investing time and expertise for a future stake.
